I am a senior undergraduate student at GIST with a deep fascination for AGI, LLM reasoning, and efficient AI inference. My research focuses on understanding how intelligent systems can improve their reasoning capabilities through interaction and adaptation.

Currently, I am conducting research on LLM uncertainty-based agentic memory at the Learning and Adaptation Algorithm Lab under Prof. Taesup Kim's supervision. I am also working on LLM with reinforcement learning at the Data Science Lab under Prof. Sundong Kim's supervision.

Through the Korea-Germany Junior Research Fellowship, I had the opportunity to work as a visiting researcher at Forschungszentrum Jülich's INM-7 institute directed by Prof. Simon B. Eickhoff, where I investigated the intersection of neuroscience and machine learning through brain connectivity analysis.
